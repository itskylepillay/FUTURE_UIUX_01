# OrcaHalo Studios – High-Conversion Local Service Website Design

## Project Overview

OrcaHalo Studios is a UI/UX design project focused on creating a professional, conversion-oriented website for a local web design agency.

The project addresses common weaknesses found in service-based business websites, including unclear messaging, poor content structure, weak calls-to-action, and inconsistent mobile experiences. The design applies professional UX principles used by agencies and product teams to create a structured, credible, and results-driven website.

This website is informative and static in nature, designed to clearly communicate services, establish trust, and guide potential clients toward contacting the agency.

---

## Business Category

Local Web Design and UI/UX Agency

OrcaHalo Studios provides digital services to small and medium-sized businesses seeking professional website design, user experience design, and brand positioning.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project was to design a modern, structured, and high-clarity website that:

* Clearly explains the agency’s services
* Establishes credibility with first-time visitors
* Showcases previous work professionally
* Presents transparent pricing information
* Guides users toward contacting the business
* Functions seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices

The focus of the design is on supporting business outcomes through structured communication and intentional user flow.

## UX and Design Decisions

### Clear Value Proposition

The hero section communicates:

* What OrcaHalo Studios does
* Who it serves
* The primary benefit of its services

This reduces confusion and improves first-impression clarity.

### Logical Information Architecture

Content is organized in a sequence that guides users naturally from introduction to services, trust elements, pricing, and contact.

### Visual Hierarchy

Typography contrast, spacing, alignment, and structured sections are used to guide attention and improve readability.

### Call-to-Action Strategy

Primary and secondary CTAs are placed:

* In the hero section
* After service explanations
* At the conclusion of key sections

This supports user movement toward inquiry.

### Trust and Credibility

Trust is reinforced through:

* Transparent pricing
* Portfolio presentation
* Team introduction
* Clean and consistent branding

### Mobile Responsiveness

The mobile layout prioritizes:

* Clear content stacking
* Readable typography
* Accessible navigation
* Proper spacing for touch interaction
